Sax-o-Phone

A saxophone you play by tilting your phone.

  1. Tap Start (enables sound & motion).
  2. Hold the phone at a comfy rest angle, tap Calibrate.
  3. Press the keys to finger a note, then tilt the top of the phone up to blow.
  4. No motion sensor? Use the big BLOW pad instead.
